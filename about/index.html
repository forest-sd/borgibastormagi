About Borgi Bastor Magi

Borgi Bastor Magi is the creative partnership of Nada Borgi and Etienne Bastormagi, a Beirut-based design duo known for their innovative approach to urban design and architecture. Their work blends minimalist aesthetics with magical inspiration, creating spaces and products that transform the everyday into the extraordinary.

Our Vision

We believe that design has the power to enchant. Our approach combines architectural precision with creative storytelling, resulting in spaces and products that captivate and inspire. From our signature fillet shelf designs to large-scale urban installations, every project reflects our commitment to beauty, functionality, and wonder.

What We Do

Our studio specializes in interior design, product design, and urban space transformation. We draw inspiration from Beirut's rich architectural heritage and the timeless allure of magical symbolism. Whether creating bespoke furniture, curating spaces, or consulting on architectural projects, we bring a unique perspective that sets our work apart.
